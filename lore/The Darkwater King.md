#npc

**Status:** Missing

The Darkwater King is one of the archfey, the patron of drowning rivers and brackish ponds and ruler of the [[Gloaming Court]]. He is the erstwhile companion of [[Orin]] when he was a humble fey frog trickster.

**History:**

1. Flibnik became the Darkwater King when he sacrificed himself at [[The Gloaming Banquet]] and donned the [[The Dimlit Regalia]] and took over the [[Gloaming Court]].
2. When The Dark Water King ascended to his throne, he lead his court on a crusade to storm the walls of [[Fairie]] and reclaim it.
3. Last seen heading towards [[Fairie]].

**Notes:**

- Possesses [[The Dimlit Necklace]] and a [[Dimlit bracelet]].
