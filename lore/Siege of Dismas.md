The final confrontation between the [[Three Kings]] and the [[Commonwealth Army]] after the [[Forward Push 2]], after which a stalemate was reached between the two forces.. The siege was brief but catastrophic.

1. [[Salt Gate]] was destroyed by [[Commonwealth Army]] artillery.
   1. Opening a hole in the [[Inner Wall]] and creating [[The Blow]].
2. The [[Company of the Black Griffin]] was disbanded by the [[Mandatory Forces]] for treachery.
   1. Betrayed by [[Captain Hyde]].
3. The [[Three Kings]] close [[Fish Gate]], [[Silver Gate]] and the [[Grand Market]] blocking all exits from [[South Dismas]].
4. The ascension of [[The Darkwater King]] caused the banks of the [[River Hy]] to breach.
   1. Magically fouled waters flood [[South Dismas]] and beyond, creating [[The Wash]].
5. Food shortages and disease spreads.
6. The [[Midday Riots]].
   1. Rioters barricade the streets around the [[Grand Market]] forming the [[Dismas Commune]].
