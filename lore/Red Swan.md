#npc

**Status:** Dead

**ATTRIBUTES:** STRENGTH 12, AGILITY 3, WITS 4, EMPATHY 5
**ARMOR RATING:** 0
**SKILLS:** DODGE 4, INSIGHT 3, LORE 1, MANIPULATION 5, MOVE 1, PERFORMANCE 2, SCOUTING 2
**GEAR:** [[Red Swans' Looking Glass]], rapier (1H +2 2 DAMAGE ARM LIGHT POINTED EDGED), 4 knives, [[Pot of black paint]], fine garments, pearl earrings (13 silver), gold ring (13 silver) and ruby necklace (10 gold)
**TALENTS:** [Witchcraft 3](https://grislyeye.com/blog/spirit-discipline-1)
**MIRROR MAGIC:** Red Swan must be in ARMS REACH or closer to a mirror to use her powers.
**WARDED:** Takes half damage from physical attacks.

> A glamorous figure, dressed in a deep-red velvet gown and matching headscarf. With her long, aquiline face and perfect pale-dark features, she seems as though she just stepped out of a portrait.

The Red Swan is a famous concubine, and secret [[Black Hand]] witch. She appears as an impossibly handsome woman, with a short temper and humourless demeanour.

She is sworn to [[Medai]]: in exchange for charm and status so that she might finally get revenge upon her father, [[King Beyr]]. [[Medai]] gives her the ability to access her Witchcraft through mirrors.

- Wears thick makeup to hide her witch's mark: a clawed handprint on her left temple, much like that of a child.
- Guilty about her dark deeds.
  - Avoids eye contact.
- **SCOUTING:** Crooked mouth, familiar to anyone who knows [[King Beyr]].
  - Illegitimate daughter of [[King Beyr]].
- Kidnapped [[Annette-Marie DuPont]] and dragged her to the [[Dismas Deadside|Dismas Deadside]].
- Keeps a string of men for her own entertainment.
- Has been offered [[King Beyr]]'s eyes and tongue on a mirrored plate by [[Privy Council]].
- Possess [[Red]] and [[Landon]]'s reflections.
