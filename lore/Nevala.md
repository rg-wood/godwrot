- **Morose.** Bored, impatient, negative, sarcastic.
- **Frightened.** Confused and defensive when asked about his powers.
  - Doesn't exactly understand what is happening to him.

* **Heartless.** Shadow corruption is making him increasingly inhuman and sociopathic.
* **Insecure.** Constantly seeks approval from [[The Merchant]].

1. [[Nevala]] is [[The Merchant]]'s bastard son.
2. [[The Merchant killed Nevala's Mother]]
