#encounter

In the middle of the night, the party are awakened by [[Droop]] sneaking around [[The Gull's Nest]].

[[Droop]] wants:

- Money for his defence fund for [[The Grapevine]] (but won't say this).
- Understand which side [[Red]] is on the conflict between [[Thieves' Council of Dismas|Thieves' Council of Dismas]] and [[Cult of the Bastard]].
- Understand [[Red]]'s feelings for [[Mother Maude]].

**Rewards:**

- Successfully negotiating with [[Droop]] prevents the doom clock moving forward during the next quarter.
- [[Droop]] reveals his suspicion of [[Munder's Hounds]].
- [[Droop]] deflects suspicion to [[Mother Maude|Maude]].
- Clue: dogs are following [[Cyssie du Duck]]
