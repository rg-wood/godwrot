**Pronouns:** they/them
**Status:** Missing in [[Faerie]]

**GEAR:** [[Flibnik's Silver Bell]]

Orin was a fletcher from beyond the [[Grey Woald]] who was accompanied by [[The Darkwater King|Flibnik]], her fairy friend, and [[River]], her faithful pet dog.

**History:**

1. Met [[Lia]] and [[Red]] at [[Red Castle Inn]].
2. Stopped at an seemingly abandoned farmhouse in [[Old Dismas]].
3. Helped negotiate passage through [[Dismas]] with the [[Chancellor of Scraps]].
4. Helped [[Femi-Descends-From-Orun]] and the [[Jinibian Concern]] to infiltrate the [[Locksmith's Guild]] and sabotage of the [[Palanthis Lock]].
5. Returned to the [[Company of the Black Griffin|Black Griffins]]' camp.
6. Left camp to bring an [[Orcling]] cadaver to [[The White Alchemist]].
7. Travelled via the [[Grey Woald]] to avoid [[Witchfinders]] and [[Roadwardens]].
8. Become embroiled with a tense argument with [[Red]] in the [[Grey Woald]], during which [[Lia]] slipped away.
9. Set out to track [[Lia]].
10. Passed through [[Skar]].
11. Fought the [[Gloaming Queen]] at [[The Gloaming Banquet]].
12. Was ambushed by [[Mandatory Forces]] lead by [[Captain Hyde]].
13. Escaped into [[Siad]] with [[Pie]], [[Agata]], [[Lia]] and [[Red]].
14. Encountered [[Lick]] and left with [[River]] and [[Agata]] to find [[The Darkwater King|Flibnik]].
