#npc

Serena is a gate keeper for the [[Dismas Night Market]] who can be found standing watch at the night gate, next to [[The Whetstone]].

Serena is notable for her **large, aquiline nose** and **sharp eyes**. She dresses in the **puffed-up finery** of a minor lord.

Serena is extremely **friendly** and welcoming, asking lots of questions and offering to show newcomers around. But, she always has a slight smile on her face as though she knows more than she says.

- ⊙ Extremely **friendly** and welcoming
  - ⊙⊙ Asks lots of questions.
- ⊙ Calls everyone "**my cock**."
- ⊙ Palms a small knife.
  - ⊙⊙ Discreetly cuts a notch on a wooden beam.
    - ◉◉ Serena is a spy for the [[Cult of the Bastard]].
      - ◉◉ Reports on everyone going in and out of the Night Market.
- ➔ Visitors to the Night Market must pay a toll (1 SP) and show a stolen item.
