#location

This large area was once a park, but has been cleared for agriculture and boasts a number of fields for crops and cattle. It is farmed by [[New Fanatics]] as a means of providing for the inhabitants of the Inner City.

- Despite the best efforts of the Bishop's fanatics, yields are low.
- The Gospels are a hotbed of dissent and Commonwealth sympathisers.
