#location

![[The Mare.jpg]]

A theatre of some international renown and no small amount of intrigue. Known for its scandalous and nigh-heretical performances.

> This horseshoe-shaped building stands three stories high, each leaning over each other to offer an increasingly improved (and thus perilous) view of the stage.

- During the day there is a 2-in-6 chance (`dice:1d6`) a rehearsal is taking place.
- At night there is a 4-in-6 chance (`dice:1d6`) a play is on (spark `dice: [[Chalk Quarter^sparks]]|Spark 1` `dice: [[Chalk Quarter^sparks]]|Spark 2`).
- Secretly a hive of [[Flock]] activity.
- [[The Sparrow]] lives here under his cover identity.
  - She keeps a hidden boudoir in the rafters filled with outfits and gowns.
