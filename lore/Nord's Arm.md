#location

[[Nord's Arm]] is a large capstan crane, one a a series used to load and offload vessels docked at the [[Sea Tharn]]. It is so large, it has become a building in its own right, houses a number of rooms. It is used by [[Munder's Hounds]] for their smuggling operations.

- Small **children** come to **gawp and cheer** at the contraption when it is in operation.
- **Stray dogs** pad about everywhere.
