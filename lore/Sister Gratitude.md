> This short woman wears a scowl on her face.

Sister Gratitude is a nun at [[Whittock Priory]], gardener, and secret keeper of the [[Library of Thorns]]. She was an ally of [[Sister Persistence]]. Stern and humourless.

**Notes:**

- Obsessed with the logic of sin.
- Wears a white rose pinned to her lapel.
- Suspects [[Sister Naive]].
- Speaks with a thick, Woald accent.
- Comes from beyond the [[Grey Woald]].
