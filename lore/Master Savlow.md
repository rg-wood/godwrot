#npc

Master of the [[Locksmith's Guild]] and acolyte of the [[Key Cult]], Savlow keeps a low profile. He has been appointed guild envoy to the [[Thieves' Council of Dismas|Thieves' Council of Dismas]], a relationship the guild very much wants kept quiet. He reports only to [[Grand Master Kater]].

⊙ Dressed in rags
⊙ Large, bushy eyebrows.
⊙ Demands all doors shut in his presence
⊙ Something silver glimmers at the collar of his shirt.
◉ Wears silver lock on chain under clothes.
