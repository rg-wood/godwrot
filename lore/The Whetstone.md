#location

This smart tavern sits on [[The Run Off]]. It's a notorious hangout for the enforcers, spies and advisors of [[Privy Council]].

- ⊙ Loud laughing and chatter.
- ⊙ Strong smell of snuff.
- ⊙ "Stokemen" guard the entrance and patrol.
  - ⊙ ⊙ Wear tall hats.
- ➔ Entrance is via a large, windowless door.
  - ➔ Non-members are not welcome.
  - ➔ Customers are welcomed by [[Hosteller Stoke]].
- ⊙ A large whetstone sits at the centre of the main bar.
  - ⊙ ⊙ It is spattered with blood.
  - ⊙ Someone is tied to the whetstone. They are casually tortured by the clientele. (Night.)
- ⊙ Outside a figure lurks at the corner (Night).
  - ➔ [[Serena the Clip]] guards the entrance to [[Dismas Night Market]].
    - ➔ Behind is a dark alleyway that leads to spiralling stairs the take you down to [[Dismas Night Market]].
