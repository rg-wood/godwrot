**Leader:** [[Queen Maug]]

The [[Ghost]]s of the Hanged Court rule the [[Dismas Deadside|Dismas Deadside]] of [[Dismas]].

The Court itself is a large dead tree that sits somewhere beneath [[Dismas]]. Ribbons, beads and bells hang from its trees. A number of high-ranking ghosts also hang here including [[Queen Maug]] herself.
