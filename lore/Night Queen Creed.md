> "I hate to be that person, but I'd like to know if I'm going to die."

The Night Queen is a **severely dressed** woman in her 50s. Her **thick glasses flash** in light, hiding her gaze behind an **implacable facade**. She masterminds the [[Thieves' Council of Dismas|Thieves' Council of Dismas]] with her **Machiavellian** command of politics.

She watches the local goings-on in the city with an obsessive eye (Dice Pool 6).

She can often be found (4-in-6 chance) in her "throne" room in the [[Dismas Night Market]].
