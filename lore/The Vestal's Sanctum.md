> A spartan, windowless room with but a few items of bare furniture that have all been pushed hard against the walls. A large, bare space has been left in the middle.

[[Sister Naive|The Vestal]] keeps a room at [[Madam Ermen's House]] which she uses to receive messages from [[Hell]]. The room appears to be a spartan bedroom whose shutters a nailed shut.

- The room has a double in [[Hell]].
  - Disquieting atmosphere.
- Voices can sometimes be heard calling for [[Sister Naive|The Vestal]] even when the room is empty.
