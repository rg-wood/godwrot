#npc

**ATTRIBUTES:** Strength 1, Agility 5, Wits 3, Empathy 0
**SKILLS:** Lore 4 (requires a successful LORE check to understand), Move 4, Scouting 5
**MOVEMENT:** 1
**CLAWS:** 1
**FAMILIAR:** Can substitute as an ingredient for any Witchcraft spell. This can stack with one other ingredient.
**SHADE SPEAKER:** [[Shade]]s must make a successful Wits check to avoid being intimidated by Sylas.
**REND VEIL:** With a successful claw attack, Sylas can open a hole into the [[Shadowside]] large enough for him to pass, which he can also use to teleport to another NEAR location. With more work Sylas can open larger holes.
