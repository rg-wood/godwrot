#location

![[Little Wharf.png]]

This bustling wharf is the hub for ferries travelling up and down the [[River Hy]]. Famous for its extremely aggressive seagulls.

- There is a 2-in-6 (`dice: 1d6`) chance of being attacked by seagulls.
- Dead seagulls hang everywhere as a warning to the others.
