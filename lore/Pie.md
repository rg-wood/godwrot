#npc

![[Pie.png|200]]

> A fair man of few years, a thin strip of a boy at most with a mad brush of dark brown hair. He wears a slightly embarrassed look on his face.

Pie young man who became involved with the [[Flock]] at an early age. None too bright and prone to confusion and high emotions. He says little and, when he does, speaks without feeling.

1. Worked for the [[Office of Duties]].
2. Secret [[Flock]] operative.
3. Published pamphlets from the [[Old Flock Safe House]]
4. Rescued from [[Old Spit's Nest]] by [[Orin]], [[Ba'raknul]] and [[Biron]].
5. Sent to the [[New Commonwealth]] by [[Orin]] but captured by the [[Company of the Black Griffin]]
6. Travelled through [[Siad]] with the party during the [[Siege of Dismas]].
7. After worked as [[The Sparrow]]'s personal assistant.
8. Recruited as leader of [[Pie's Cell]].
9. Beginning to become more confident as a [[Flock]] agent.
