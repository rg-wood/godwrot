**LIGHTNING FAST.** Raul always draws first during the initiative draw.

> A short man, young but haggardly thin and wiry. His scrawny hands are caked white with chalk. It smudges the rest of his face and clothes.

Raul the Sharp (or just "Sharp") is a [[Cult of the Bastard]] left-hand, who believes a fair fight is blasphemy, and who delights in a low blow or knife in the back.

- **Tolerant.** Just as all are welcome to hide in the Bastard's shadow, all are welcome to Raul's help.
- **Chalky.** Always carries chalk to illustrate his points. It smudges everything he touches.
- **Infirmity.** Raul paid the Bastard's price with his stamina, and suffers from a permanent tremble which betrays his ability to fight.
- **Scornful.** Considers [[Mother Maude]] to be a dangerous fanatic.

**History:**

1. Drank poison to become a left-hand.
2. Wandered the [[Midlands]] as an itinerant left-hand during the [[Woreham Stalemate]].
3. Was attracted to [[Dismas]] after learning of [[Negotiation between the Grapevine and the Council]].
4. Impressed with [[Droop]]'s ruthless insouciance has begun preparing him to become a left-hand to replace [[Mother Maude]].
5. Advocated against the violence of [[The Grapevines attack on the Night Market]].
6. Pickpocketed the [[Letter from Droop to The Merchant]] from [[Square]].
