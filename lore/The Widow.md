**ATTRIBUTES:** STRENGTH 12, AGILITY 4, WITS 3
**ARMOUR RATING:** 0
**MOVEMENT:** 1
**SKILLS:** Endurance 2, Might 4, Stealth 4, Move 3, Lore 2,
**GEAR:** Small book with a list of her kills, bone saw, pot of black paint.
**TALENTS:** Path of Death 3, Witchcraft 3
**WARDED:** Takes half damage from physical attacks.

This [[Black Hand]] witch is a true monster, a psychopath that walks and talks like a normal human, but has been so warped by her ruinous magics she has lost all humanity.

The Widow has two guises: glamoured to appear as [[The Countess|Baroness Malignon]], and, by day, [[The Widow|Mrs Simms]]:

> A short, unassuming women wearing the severe, black garments of an undertaker. Her eyes are red-rimmed and blood shot, as though she has just been crying.

- **Sea-tainted.** Always smells faintly of fish and brine.
- **Eager.** Seemingly impervious to threats or insults, just nods or shakes her head eagerly.
- **Hungry.** Eager to kill as many people as she can.
- **Book of the Dead.** Keeps a small book on her person, listing all the people she has killed.

## History

1. The Widow travelled from [[Port Red Sand]] to [[Faro]] aboard [[The Tempest]] as a young girl.
2. [[The Tempest]] encountered a storm that killed everyone aboard.
3. [[The Widow]] cut a deal with [[Hell]] so that she was the only survivor of [[The Tempest]].
4. [[The Tempest]] washed ashore at [[Dismas]].
5. [[The Plenary]] scolded the Widow for her lack of subtlety.
6. She grows a grudge against [[The Plenary]].
