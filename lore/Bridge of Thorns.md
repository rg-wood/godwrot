#location

![[Bridge of Thorns.png]]

The forbidding bridge of the [[River Ly]]. It is named after the rows of heads on pikes that look over the waters.

- At night it is common to hear the whispers of [[Thieves' Council of Dismas|Thieves' Council of Dismas]] gate keepers below.
- Locals think its haunted.
- Guarded by [[Vultain]].
