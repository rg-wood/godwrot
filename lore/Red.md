#pc

**Player:** Kat
**Pronouns:** she/her

**Red** is a ruffian, an orphan from the slums of [[Kastenburg]]. She's a hulking woman with pale skin, shoulder-length dark-red hair and the stocky frame of a farmhand. Dour, glum and not a little bit suspicious, she makes a loyal friend once her trust can be earned.

**Notes:**

- Fiercely protective if [[Lia]].
- [[Maude wants Red Back]]
- Feels responsible for [[Maisey]]
- [[Mother Maude]] revealed the location of [[Red's Mother's Lodgings]]

**History:**

1. Grew up in [[The Grapevine]], at the time a half-way house for waifs and urchins in [[Kastenburg]].
2. Encountered [[Lia]] when she was sent to intimidate [[The Grapevine]].
3. [[The Grapevine]] was disbanded when members were charged with treason.
4. [[Red]] spent many years in prison.
5. When released, with no place to call home, she fled [[Kastenburg]] with [[Lia]].
6. Travelled for a while.
7. Joined up with the [[Company of the Black Griffin]].
8. Was sent to the [[Red Castle Inn]] to meet with [[Orin]] and [[Quinn]].
9. Stopped at an seemingly abandoned farmhouse in [[Old Dismas]].
10. Helped negotiate a deal with the [[Chancellor of Scraps]].
11. Helped [[Femi-Descends-From-Orun]] and the [[Jinibian Concern]] to infiltrate the [[Locksmith's Guild]] and sabotage of the [[Palanthis Lock]].
12. Returned to the [[Company of the Black Griffin|Black Griffins]]' camp.
13. Left camp to bring an [[Orcling]] cadaver to [[The White Alchemist]].
14. Travelled via the [[Grey Woald]] to avoid [[Witchfinders]] and [[Roadwardens]].
15. Become embroiled with a tense argument with [[Orin]], during which [[Lia]] slipped away.
16. Set out to track [[Lia]].
17. Caught [[Skar Cough]] in [[Skar]].
18. Fought the [[Gloaming Queen]] at [[The Gloaming Banquet]].
19. Was ambushed by [[Mandatory Forces]] lead by [[Captain Hyde]].
20. Escaped into [[Siad]] with [[Pie]], [[Agata]], [[Lia]] and [[Orin]].
21. Encountered [[Lick]] who grants them passage back to [[Dismas]].
22. Was abducted by [[The Sparrow]] during the [[Siege of Dismas]].
23. Joined the [[Flock]].
