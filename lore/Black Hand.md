#faction

**Symbol:** A black handprint.
**Leader:** [[The Plenary]]

A coven of witches and assassins commanded by [[The Plenary]]. There are only ever five members of the Black Hand:

- [[Red Swan|The Concubine]] who maintains relations in the spiritual words.
- [[The Countess]] who maintains relations in the physical world.
- [[The Plenary]] who leads.
- [[Sister Naive|The Vestal]] who acts as speaker with [[Hell]].
- [[The Widow]] who assassinates Hell's enemies.

To join an existing member must die or be killed to make an opening.
