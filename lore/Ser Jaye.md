**Pronouns:** she/her

**ATTRIBUTES:** Strength 3, Agility 3, Wits 4, Empathy 2
**SKILLS:** Endurance 2, Lore 2, Melee 1, Marksmanship 2
**MOVEMENT:** 1
**MATCHLOCK PISTOL:** Damage 1, Short Range, Loading
**INTIMIDATING:** Roll +1 when threatening someone. If she succeeds, her opponent cannot demand anything in return from her. They can still choose to attack you.

> A young woman in fine clothes, wearing thick gloves, a bright, red scarf and has long locks of red hair that fall over her shoulder. She wears a wide-brimmed witchfinder's hat at a jaunty angle.

Ser Jaye is a Knight of the Well and senior [[Witchfinders|Witchfinder]] with a cruel smile and sharp wit. She is usually accompanied by personal bodyguard, [[Remember-Well]] and her pet witch, [[Gosling]], whose chain she holds.

- **Private.** Wears gloves, scarf and wig to hide burn marks.
- **Cruel.** The pain of her burns is driving her to cruel japes and sadism.
  - **Addicted.** Huffs acrid pipe tobacco.

**History:**

1. Burn victim of [[The Snadeforth Witch]].
