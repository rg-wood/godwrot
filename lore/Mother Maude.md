> An elderly women with a drooping face, a crown of ghostly-white hair and a frail form. She has a nasty case of the shakes. Two gold coins cover her eyes.

Maude was once a kindly old maid who ran [[The Grapevine]] as a halfway house for orphans and urchins.

All that changed when she was arrested for treason over supposed links to [[New Commonwealth]]. While rotting away in a [[Kastenburg]] gaol she received a visitation from [[ðŸ—¡ Dismas Remade/The Bastard|The Bastard]]. Feeling her rage and despair, he offered her a deal.

Maude's luck soon changed. Somehow her sentence was commuted and she found herself a free woman. At that, she set about finding her lost pets and reassembling the Grapevine.

**Notes:**

- Maude is very elderly and not long for this world.
- Maude fears for [[Droop]]'s anger but is too **guilty** to do anything directly about it.
- Maude wants [[Red]] to take over [[The Grapevine]].
