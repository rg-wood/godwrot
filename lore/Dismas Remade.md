#campaign #low-fantasy

> Whereupon our scoundrels are caught in the midst of a secret war. They'll serve mysterious masters, often at great peril. For what? Perhaps to repay a debt. Maybe a chance at a better life. Or just to survive.

[Dismas Remade](https://dismas.grislyeye.com/) is a [[Folkloric fantasy]] campaign set in the city of [[Dismas]]. The party are agents of the [[Flock]], working undercover in a secret war against the [[Privy Council]].
