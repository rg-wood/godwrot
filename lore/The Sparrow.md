_He/she_ #npc

> A young, good-looking man with hair short, almost to the scalp. His skin is pale, fine and bland as porcelain. Otherwise, his features are vaguely bland and forgettable.

The Sparrow is the **spy master** behind the [[Flock]], and a **master of disguise**. **Highly strung**, easily annoyed and **mischievous**.

- Driven to peacefully liberate [[Dismas]] from the [[Three Kings]].
- An actor by trade.
- Still performs as cover for his espionage activities.
- Ensures everything happens on his terms.

**History:**

1. Began as a young catamite.
2. [[Madam Lizbetta]] was his madam.
3. Disguised as Corporal Linus, she helped the party rescue [[Pie]].
4. Offered to use the [[Flock]] to bring [[South Dismas]] over to the [[New Commonwealth]] peacefully, and stop the advance of the [[Commonwealth Army]].
5. Recruited [[Lia]] and [[Red]].
6. The [[High Chamber]] has grown impatient and suspicious of progress with the attempts to overthrow [[South Dismas]].
7. A bounty has been placed on [[The Sparrow]]'s head.
8. [[The Sparrow]] recently just survived an assassination attempt. He secretly carries a broken rib.
