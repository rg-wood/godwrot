[[Mr Kett]] is a sadistic butcher who acts as [[The Merchant]]'s secret agent in [[Dismas]]. He runs [[Kett's Pie Shop]], which he uses as a cover for his operations.

[[Mr Kett]] is a lonely killer who has a passion for serving human flesh in exotic ways. He is only frightened of [[The Merchant]] and is keen to please him.

When encountered he is `dice: [[Mr Kett#^doing]]|Doing`.

| --- | ------------------------------------------- |
| 1   | Closing up shop early.                      |
| 2   | Nursing a slash wound to his hand.          |
| 3   | Carefully cooking up a strange pie.         |
| 4   | Chopping up an indeterminate piece of meat. |
