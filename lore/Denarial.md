#npc _(they/them)_

> A great winged figure towers eight paces tall, unnaturally thin, their uneven form swaying gently for balance.

Denarial is a fallen angel, once Keeper of the South Wind, and the architect of the [[Riverhithe Accord]]. It is their responsibility to keep the [[Damned Reckoning]].

After millennia they have grown weary of their task and have come to realise they must not only count the sinners in hell, but all possible sinners, living, dead, and yet to be born, and so they seek to open the gate from [[Hell]] to the world of the living so they can finally be free of their eternal task.

- **Putrid.** Smells powerfully of soured milk.

**History:**

1. Created as Keeper of the South Wind by [[The Father]].
2. Rebelled against [[The Father]].
3. Absence caused the far southern lands to be consumed in an icy waste.
4. Appointed Damned Clerk, responsible for the [[Damned Reckoning]].
5. Negotiated the [[Riverhithe Accord]].
