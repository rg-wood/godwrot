#location

City blackmarket and [[Thieves' Council of Dismas|Thieves' Council of Dismas]] stronghold. All manner of **criminals lurk** here and **anything can be had** for the right price. Only the laws of the Council apply here, in particular the White Law which effectively **prohibits all crime**.

The market is **crowded** day and night. The **warm air** chokes with **greasy smoke**, making everyone sweat unpleasantly.

#### 4. The Market Itself

The beating heart of the Night Market, a bazaar filled with stolen and illegal goods and items.

⊙ 5-in-6 chance they have a particular item.
⊙ Items are obviously illegal: roll [[Cover and Infamy]] when used in public.
⊙ All items are half-price.
⊙ 2-in-6 chance an item is faulty (-1 Gear Bonus).
➔ Up: Stone arch carved with hands: Night gate (locked and guarded by 2 [[Ruffians]]) up to [[Bridge of Thorns]].
➔ North: Behind a waterfall sits the The Soaked Head (9).
