#location

Troigard, or the Third Gate, is an occult saloon and infamous meeting place for the [[Half-World]]. It moves from place to place in the [[Silver Gate District]], renting rooms behind or above inconspicuous businesses. The location is only revealed to notable members of the Half-World and is strictly invite-only.

- [[Just Benedict]] is banned from the Troigard.
- Triggers [[Veronica Dan Makes a Divine Offer]]
