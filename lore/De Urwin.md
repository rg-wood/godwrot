> A handsome young man, dressed tastefully, with pale skin and a pouting mouth that always seems to sneer slightly. He carries a steel cane around with him everywhere.

- Has excellent taste.
- Is the [[Red Swan]]'s current paramour.
- [[Red Swan]] knows he's cheating on her with [[Florid Doris]].
- His steel cane conceals a rapier.
