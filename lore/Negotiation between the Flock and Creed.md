#encounter

The party negotiate with [[Night Queen Creed]] on behalf of [[Flock]] for sanctuary in the [[Dismas Night Market]].

**Actors:** [[Night Queen Creed]], [[Flea]], [[Assassin]]

**Negotiation standing:**

- +1 You have helped your opponent previously.
- -1 Your opponent has more people on their side.
- -1 You ask for something valuable or dangerous.
- ~~-1 Your opponent is suspicious of you.~~
  (see _FLPH_ pg. 99-100)

**Council Goals:**

- Gain access to North Dismas.
- Defeat [[The Grapevine]].

| Offer                                            | Counteroffer                                   |
| ------------------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------- |
| Flock: 1000 GP                                   | Council: 1200 GP                               |
| Council: 1000 GP + Information Sharing (One way) | Flock: 1000 GP + Information Sharing (Two way) |
| Flock: 1000 GP + Destroy [[The Grapevine]]       | Agreed                                         |

Following the successful negotiations [[Night Queen Creed]] has decreed the following:

> By order of Creed, Night Queen of the struggling city of Dismas:
>
> 1st. Nary a thief shall molest agents of the [[Flock]] whilst upon the [[Dismas Night Market]].
>
> 2nd. As it is, no thief shall reveal the identity of an agent of the [[Flock]], elsewise.
>
> Thrd. Converaly, none of the [[Flock]] will partake any action that willingly leads to the arrest of a thief.
>
> Forth. 'Pon a thieves' moon a meeting shall be held betwixt Council and [[Flock]], to facilitate the exchanges of information.
>
> Lastly. Informal exchanges can be agreed, with a capitan mediating, if so required.
