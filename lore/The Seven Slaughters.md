A series of seven murders that occurred over a short period of time. All the victims were members of [[Kastenburg Cartel]] who died without any obvious wounds on their bodies and shadowy marks on their flesh.

- Witchcraft was suspected.
- Investigated by [[Gerrin]] under the supervision of [[Witchfinder Marcys Brekkan]].
- They were apparently committed by [[Lia]] when she blacked out.
