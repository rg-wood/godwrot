#npc

**STATS:** [[Forbidden Lands Book of Beasts.pdf#page=52&selection=122,0,122,3|Imp]]
**SKILLS:** Stealth 2, Lore 3, Insight 1, Manipulation 4
**REGENERATIVE:** Regains D3 Strength every round.
**BLASPHEMOUS:** Cannot stand the sight of holy symbols and must move away from them. If unable to do so, takes D3 damage every round.

![[Sylas.png|200]]

> A cherubic boy with golden curls and rosy cheeks. He wears the livery of the Privy Council, with a the sigil of red eagle with three heads worn across his chest. Worn so well, so perfectly turned out with a collar a brilliant white.
>
> He has an impish smile on his face, as though thinking of some private joke. You can't help feeling a smile prick your own lips.

Sylas is an [[Imp]], possessing the body of a small child. He was summoned to serve [[Lord Roche]] and act as his intermediary with [[Hell]].

**Notes:**

- Permanent smile.
- Never gets dirty.
- Speaks lumpily as though he has marbles in his mouth.
- Anyone looking closely at his mouth can make a SCOUTING check to notice he has a forked tongue.
- Always has a shadow hovering behind his shoulders.
