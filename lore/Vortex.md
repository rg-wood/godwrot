#encounter

A vortex is a swirling, sucking maelstrom of pure darkness that pulls anything caught in it's grasp 3d6 (`dice:2d6`) sink points into the [[Abyss]]. Anyone NEAR to an open vortex must make a MIGHT roll or are sucked in. There is a 3-in-6 (`dice:1d6`) a [[Nethermental]] will appear through the vortex.
