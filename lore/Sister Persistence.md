#npc

**Status:** Dead

> An elderly anchorite with a wrinkled face covered in a thick, white stubble. Large, tired eyes.

Severe nun and [[New Commonwealth]] loyalist. Fond of slapping idle hands and notable for her impressive stubble.

**History:**

1. Defected from the [[Cathedrale Temporal]] to the [[New Commonwealth]].
2. Bearer of the [[Second Seal]].
3. Met with [[Lia]], [[Red]] and [[Annette-Marie DuPont|Annette]] at her rendezvous in [[Dreyside]].
4. Ambushed [[Privy Council]] agents.
5. Was fatally wounded by [[The Countess]].
