This reclusive abbey is dedicated to [[The Mother]] and overseen by [[Sister Naive]]. It is sheltered by a locked gate and high wall, removing it from the slums that surround it. The nuns here are of a scholarly bent and the priory boasts an impressive library.

- Breathlessly quiet.
- The Priory has three hidden libraries: [[Thorn Library]], The [[Second Library]] within the [[Whittock Library]] and the [[??? Third Library]].
- Unaccompanied strangers are asked to leave.

**Locations:**

- **Gardens:** well-kept and peaceful.
  - 3-in-6 (`dice: 1d6`, day) chance to encounter [[Sister Gratitude]] gardening.
  - 2-in-6 (`dice:1d6`, day) chance [[Sisters Well, Nascence and Whelmed]] are here.
- [[Thorn Maze]]
- **Mother's Shrine:** Detailed sculpture made of marble. It is half-sunken in the ground.
  - Digging the ground around the sculpture reveals a brass plaque with a map to the [[Thorn Maze]], the [[Second Library]] and the [[??? Third Library]].
- **Main Building:** Deafening silence where nun's reflect. 1-in-6 (`dice: 1d6`) chance to encounter [[Sister Gratitude]] during the day.
  - **Refectory:** 3-in-6 (`dice:1d6`, day) chance [[Sisters Well, Nascence and Whelmed]] are here.
    - **Kitchen:** Always busy. Strong smell of herbs.
  - [[Whittock Library]]
    - [[Second Library]].
- **Dormitory:** Strangers not allowed.
  - [[Sister Naive's Quarters]]
- **Chapel:** Cluttered, acrid smell.
  - 2-in-6 (`dice:1d6`, day) chance a funeral is taking place attended by [[The Widow]].
