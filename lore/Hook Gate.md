#location

> A vast gatehouse that stands astride the Dismas streets, guarding the ghosts of walls gone as they city ever expands.

Hook Gate serves as the quarters for the city's various excisemen and clerks, who carefully track taxes, imports and exports.

**Notes:**

- Contains a debtors jail for those who cannot pay the [[Three Kings]] levies.

**History**

1. Once separated Dismas proper from the [[South Docks]]
2. After [[Salt Gate]] was destroyed in the [[Siege of Dismas]] the various clerks and excise men moved here.
