#location

The area of desolation created when the Inner Wall between the [[Chalk Quarter]] and the [[South Docks]] was destroyed during the [[Siege of Dismas]].

- The area is regularly patrolled by [[Mandatory Forces]].
- Too large to be completely surveilled.
- Rumoured to be haunted.
- A group of refugee orphans live hidden in the ruins here.
