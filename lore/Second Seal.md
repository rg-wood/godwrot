#item

One of the seals detailed in the [[Plutonus doctrine]]. It could be found at "the crossroads", according to the annotations in [[Sister Persistence's Dictum]].

**History:**

1. Was carried by [[Sister Persistence]].
2. Was stolen by [[The Countess]]
3. Was broken by [[Lord Roche]].
4. The [[Balestar]] appeared when the seal was broken.
