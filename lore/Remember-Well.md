**SKILLS:** Might 1, Lore 1, Melee 3, Marksmanship 3
**MOVEMENT:** 1
**DUAL-WIELDED MATCHLOCK PISTOLS:** Damage 1, Short Range, Loading. Extra attack per round as a fast action, with -2 modifier.

![[Remember-Well.png|200]]

> A large wall of a man, handsome and bland. Dressed head-to-foot in the stiff, black fashion of the New Fanatics. He carries a large number of flintlocks about his person.

Remember-Well is a [[Witchfinders|Witchfinder]] and loyal bodyguard to [[Ser Jaye]]. He was a lawyer before he herd the call of the witchfinders, and is a painful stickler for the law, given to long, legal diatribes. Otherwise, he is monosyllabic.

- **Quietly Competent.** Veteran of the [[Commoner Revolution]].
- **Troubled.** Beginning to realise witch-hunting is cruel.
- **Fervent.** Executes his duties with the single-mindedness of the fanatic.
